About Zoombox

The ZoomBox project officially began development on the 1st of May 2020 with the objective of creating a modern, enjoyable and functional lyrics collection website as a course assignment.


With that in mind, the site now hosts songs of multiple varieties from Metal to Pop and from English to Japanese in order to make sure anyone can enjoy using the site.


Through the cooperation of 4 students, the site has finally been finished after 22 days of development.

Zoombox is a database-less
lyrics collection developed by
4 college students.
